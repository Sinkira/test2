~ About SeeleLand ~

SeeleLand is a Seele fan community established and named after Seele in 2020.

Much like our favorite girl, the land is filled with wholesomeness and positivity. It's the home of the sweetest Seelemains who treat each other like family.

Whether you want to chat, share fanart, play games, or just have fun, SeeleLand is the place for you. There is something for everyone here.

It's a peaceful place where you will always feel welcomed and accepted.
